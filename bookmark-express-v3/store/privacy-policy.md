# Bookmark Express — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 4, 2026_

Bookmark Express is a browser extension that lets you search and open your
Chrome bookmarks. Protecting your privacy is simple here, because the extension
does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.

## What the extension accesses

- **Your bookmarks.** The extension reads your Chrome bookmarks locally in order
  to search them and open them. Your bookmarks never leave your device and are
  never sent to us or to any third party.
- **A local usage count.** To rank the bookmarks you open most often near the
  top of the results, the extension keeps a count of how many times you have
  opened each bookmark from the extension. This count is stored locally on your
  device using Chrome's `storage` API and is never transmitted anywhere.
- **Favicons.** The extension displays each bookmarked site's icon using
  Chrome's built-in favicon service. No browsing data is sent to us in the
  process.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.
- It does not transmit any data off your device.
- It does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- It does not run any remote or externally hosted code.
- It does not sell or share data with third parties.

## Data retention and removal

All data the extension stores (the per-bookmark usage counts) lives only in your
browser's local storage. Removing the extension deletes this data.
